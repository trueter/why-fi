How to use the Coffee Machine

Errors

  • Refill water tank: Lift the left lid on the side and carefully pour water into the big opening
  • Refill coffee beans: Insert coffee on the top, opening the transparent lid. Never use instant coffee
  • Empty coffee grounds: to EMPTY it you have to carefully slide the central part on top of the bottom bottom tray (where liquid is collected) towards you and empty the coffee grounds in the food waste bin. Careful: It might spill. Empty the contents
  • ?? : Empty the wastewater tray below the ground coffee tray into the sink next to the showers. Put coffee grounds into food waste

Coffee Instructions

  • Please only use coffee beans and never powder / instant coffee
  • Press the button on the top right to turn the machine on and check the display. Does it show an error? See section below.
  • Select the coffee you need: Espresso or Coffee // Top left or right button next to display
  • You can also have hot water only, but be mindful that hot water will come up from the tube on the right
  • Danger: While the machine supports foaming, it requires additional equipment to be attached to the machine, so please don't order a cappuchino unless you are certain the milk setup is properly configured.
  • Enjoy your ☕️
