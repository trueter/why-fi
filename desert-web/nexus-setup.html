Setting Up Nexus Chat on Your Mobile Device

Download & Install

First, ensure your device is connected to our local intranet Wi-Fi. Then, download the Nexus Chat app from our internal app store or directly from this link.

Configuration

  • Open the Nexus Chat app once installed.
  • Enter your provided user credentials (username and password).
  • Configure your chat settings according to the guide provided within the app.
  • If you encounter any issues, refer to the troubleshooting section below or contact IT support.

Troubleshooting

If you have any problems during the setup process, try the following:

  • Ensure you're connected to the correct Wi-Fi network.
  • Restart the Nexus Chat app.
  • Reinstall the app if the problem persists.
